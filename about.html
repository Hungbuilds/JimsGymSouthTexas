Beyond The Weight Floor

Our Community

Jim's Gym has been part of Carrizo Springs for 15 years. Along the way, we've had visitors from a lot further than Dimmit County — and some of them left something behind.

A Flag On Our Wall

The New Jersey National Guard

During a stretch in 2023–2024, members of the New Jersey National Guard training in the area made Jim's Gym their regular stop. Before they moved on, they signed and left us this flag — it's hung on our wall ever since as a small reminder of who's passed through Carrizo Springs and trained alongside our regulars.

We're proud to have hosted them, and grateful for the work they do.
