Accessibility Statement

Jim's Gym is committed to making our facility and this website usable by everyone, including people with disabilities.

This Website

We aim to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA where reasonably practicable — including sufficient color contrast, keyboard-navigable links and buttons, and descriptive alt text on images.
