Built for This Community

For 15 years, Jim's Gym has been Carrizo Springs' only 24/7 gym — the place oilfield workers coming off odd shifts, families, and lifelong locals all train, at whatever hour works for them.

No Corporate Contracts

This is a locally owned gym, not a franchise. No corporate contracts, no upsells — just real equipment (TROY dumbbells, Arsenal Strength machines) and a straightforward way to train.

A Flag Signed by the People Who Trained Under It

On the wall of the weight room hangs a New Jersey National Guard flag, signed by service members who've trained at Jim's Gym over the years. It's stayed up as a small thank-you to everyone who's served — and a reminder of who's walked through these doors.

Dimmit County's Gym

Carrizo Springs is a small town, and the nearest other gym is a long drive. Jim's Gym exists so nobody in Dimmit County has to choose between working odd hours and staying consistent with training.

Come see it for yourself — open 24/7, no appointment needed.
